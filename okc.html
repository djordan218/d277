Oklahoma City

Oklahoma City is the capital and largest city of the state of Oklahoma. Here are some details:

Population: 655,000 (approx.)

Year Incorporated: 1890

Region: Central Oklahoma

Classification: Urban

Average Income Level: $55,000 (higher than the state average)

About Oklahoma City

Oklahoma City, known for its cowboy culture and rich history, is a vibrant metropolitan area. It is the largest city in Oklahoma and serves as a cultural and economic hub.
